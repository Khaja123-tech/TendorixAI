## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Business Context
The tender and procurement industry faces significant challenges in efficiency, accuracy, and document preparation. Companies spend excessive time manually searching for relevant tenders and creating professional tender documents, often missing opportunities due to resource constraints and inefficient processes.

### 1.2 Business Opportunity
Tendorix addresses these challenges by providing an AI-powered platform that combines intelligent tender matching with automated document generation, enabling businesses to:
- Reduce tender discovery time by 80%
- Increase tender application success rates by 40%
- Accelerate document preparation by 70%
- Expand market reach through comprehensive tender coverage

### 1.3 Solution Overview
Tendorix is a comprehensive platform offering:
1. **AI-Powered Tender Matching**: Intelligent discovery and scoring of relevant tender opportunities
2. **TenderDraft Document Generation**: Automated creation of professional tender documents
3. **Company Profile Management**: Comprehensive business capability profiling
4. **Analytics and Insights**: Performance tracking and optimization recommendations

### 1.4 Business Impact
- **Revenue Growth**: 25-40% increase in tender win rates
- **Cost Reduction**: 60-80% reduction in tender research and document preparation time
- **Market Expansion**: Access to previously undiscovered tender opportunities
- **Competitive Advantage**: First-mover advantage in AI-powered tender management

## 8. Business Rules

### 8.1 User Management Rules

#### BR-001: Account Creation
- Users must provide valid email address for registration
- Email verification is required before account activation
- Password must meet security requirements (8+ characters, mixed case, numbers)
- One account per email address
- Company information is required for full platform access

#### BR-002: Profile Management
- Company profile must be 80%+ complete for tender matching
- Financial information is required for tender value filtering
- Profile updates trigger re-evaluation of existing matches
- Profile data is validated against external sources where possible

### 8.2 Tender Matching Rules

#### BR-003: Matching Criteria
- Minimum 60% matching score required for tender recommendations
- Geographic preferences are strictly enforced
- Financial capacity must meet tender requirements
- Industry sector alignment is weighted at 30% of total score
- Experience requirements are mandatory filters

#### BR-004: Notification Rules
- High-priority matches (85%+ score) trigger immediate notifications
- Daily digest emails for medium-priority matches (70-84% score)
- Weekly summary for all matching activity
- Users can customize notification preferences
- Deadline alerts sent 48 hours and 24 hours before submission

### 8.3 Document Generation Rules

#### BR-005: Template Processing
- Only DOCX format templates are supported
- Templates must contain valid field placeholders
- Maximum template size is 10MB
- Template parsing must complete within 60 seconds
- Invalid templates are rejected with detailed error messages

#### BR-006: Field Mapping Rules
- Automatic mapping requires 70%+ confidence score
- Manual review required for 50-69% confidence mappings
- Fields with <50% confidence are marked as unmapped
- Required fields must be mapped before document generation
- Mapping preferences are learned and applied to future documents

### 8.4 Data Management Rules

#### BR-007: Data Retention
- User data is retained for account lifetime plus 7 years
- Generated documents are stored for 5 years
- Audit logs are retained for 7 years
- Deleted accounts are anonymized after 30 days
- Backup data is retained for 1 year

#### BR-008: Data Privacy
- Personal data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Data access is logged and monitored
- Users can request data export at any time
- Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days
- Third-party data sharing requires explicit consent
